Logistics Agent — Autonomous Operations AI

An autonomous AI agent that manages freight bookings, tracks shipments in real time, and re-routes around disruptions — keeping your goods moving without constant human intervention.

The Problem It Solves

Logistics teams spend hours chasing carrier updates, manually booking shipments, and firefighting exceptions. Disruptions are discovered via customer complaints, not early-warning systems.

Shipment visibility gaps lead to reactive firefighting
Carrier booking done manually across multiple portals
Exception handling consumes 50%+ of team capacity
3–5 days
Average visibility lag on in-transit shipments

How It Works

See the full workflow on Digital Workforce → Workflows

Step 1

Book

Selects optimal carrier and lane based on cost, speed, and reliability score.

Step 2

Track

Monitors shipment at event level — not just "departed / arrived".

Step 3

Alert

Flags delays, customs holds, or route anomalies before they escalate.

Step 4

Re-route

Proposes or executes alternative routing for at-risk shipments.

Capabilities

Multi-carrier booking and rate comparison
Real-time event-level shipment tracking
Predictive delay scoring per shipment
Automated exception alerting with severity triage
Alternative routing proposals with cost impact
Freight invoice validation and dispute flagging

Data Sources

TMS and carrier APIs
Port congestion and weather feeds
Customs clearance status
Carrier performance history
Sales orders and delivery commitments

Output

Carrier booking confirmations
Live shipment status dashboard
Exception alert with recommended action
Re-routing proposals (auto or human-approved)
Freight cost and performance analytics
