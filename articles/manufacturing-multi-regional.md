## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
China+1 strategy is no longer sufficient for risk mitigation.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Manufacturing is shifting to multi-region redundancy models.

## Impact (So what?)
Higher cost but lower risk. Improved regional responsiveness.

## Action (What companies should do)
Replicate production lines across regions. Build multi-node networks.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Manufacturing is becoming regionally distributed.
