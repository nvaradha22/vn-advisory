## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Global supply chains assumed stable globalization. That assumption is no longer valid.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Supply chains are fragmenting into regional blocs. Redundancy is replacing efficiency.

## Impact (So what?)
Higher sourcing complexity. Multi-region production expansion. Increased compliance constraints.

## Action (What companies should do)
Build regional supplier networks. Avoid single-region dependency. Diversify manufacturing hubs.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Supply chains are becoming regional ecosystems.
