## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
CBAM has entered enforcement in 2026, shifting carbon from reporting to pricing. Energy-intensive exports are now directly exposed to carbon cost adjustments at EU borders.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Carbon is now embedded into trade pricing. Supply chains are being audited across all tiers. Competitiveness is shifting toward low-carbon production systems.

## Impact (So what?)
Export margins reduce for high-carbon goods. Energy sourcing becomes strategic. Carbon reporting becomes mandatory in trade flows.

## Action (What companies should do)
Map product-level emissions. Transition to renewable energy sources. Redesign supplier ecosystems based on carbon intensity.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Carbon efficiency is now a core determinant of global competitiveness.
