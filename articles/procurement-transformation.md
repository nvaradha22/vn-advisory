## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Procurement is still viewed as a cost control function.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Procurement is becoming data-driven and risk-aware. Supplier ecosystems are dynamic.

## Impact (So what?)
Supplier risk becomes critical. Procurement drives resilience.

## Action (What companies should do)
Digitize procurement workflows. Build supplier intelligence systems.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Procurement is becoming a strategic intelligence engine.
