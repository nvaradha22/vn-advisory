## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Lean systems optimize efficiency but fail under volatility.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Operations are shifting toward adaptive resilience models.

## Impact (So what?)
More flexible systems. Faster recovery from disruptions.

## Action (What companies should do)
Design modular production systems. Diversify suppliers.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Adaptive operations replace static efficiency models.
