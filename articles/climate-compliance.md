## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Climate regulation is becoming a structural supply chain driver.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Compliance is now embedded in supply chain design decisions.

## Impact (So what?)
Higher compliance cost. Energy sourcing becomes strategic.

## Action (What companies should do)
Implement carbon visibility systems. Integrate ESG into procurement.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Compliance is now structural, not optional.
