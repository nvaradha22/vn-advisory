## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Traditional supply chains rely on delayed planning cycles and reactive execution models.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
AI is merging planning and execution into a continuous decision system. Supply chains become adaptive networks.

## Impact (So what?)
Faster response times. Reduced inventory inefficiency. Automated optimization of logistics.

## Action (What companies should do)
Integrate unified data systems. Deploy predictive analytics. Automate low-risk decisions.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Supply chains are evolving into autonomous execution networks.
