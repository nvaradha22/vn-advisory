## Context / Problem (Why this matters)
Supply chain risk directly impacts financial performance.

## Deep Insight (What is changing)
Operational disruptions now affect cash flow and valuation.

## Impact (So what?)
Risk becomes a financial planning variable.

## Action (What companies should do)
Integrate finance and supply chain systems. Build scenario models.

## Conclusion (Strategic takeaway)
Supply chain risk is financial risk.
