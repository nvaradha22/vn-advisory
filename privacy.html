Privacy Policy

How we handle personal information submitted through this website. Written in plain language. Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

VN Advisory is a boutique advisory practice based in Chennai, India, providing supply chain, procurement, and AI strategy advisory services. In this policy, "we", "our", and "the practice" refer to VN Advisory. "You" refers to anyone visiting this website or submitting an enquiry.

For data protection purposes, VN Advisory is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. Contact details are at the bottom of this page.

What information we collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to provide. Specifically:

  • Contact form submissions: first name, last name, email address, company name, area of interest, and the content of any message you write.
  • Direct email correspondence: if you write to us at info@vnadvisory.in or varadha@vnadvisory.in, we receive your email address and the content of your message.
  • Diagnostic tool answers: we do not collect these. The supply chain diagnostic on the contact page runs entirely in your browser. Your answers are not transmitted to us or stored anywhere.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or third-party advertising trackers on this website at this time. If we add analytics in future, this policy will be updated and you will be informed in advance through a clear notice on the site.

Why we collect it

We collect personal information for one purpose: to respond to your enquiry and, where appropriate, to discuss whether we can help you with an advisory engagement.

Under GDPR terminology, our lawful basis for processing is your consent (when you submit a form) and legitimate interest (responding to a business enquiry you initiated). Under India's DPDP Act, the basis is your consent given at the point of submission.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information for as long as is reasonably necessary to handle your request and any subsequent commercial relationship that may follow. Specifically:

  • If you do not engage us as an advisor: we delete your enquiry information within 24 months of the last contact, unless you ask us to delete it sooner.
  • If you become a client: we keep records for the duration of the engagement and for the period required by applicable Indian tax and contract law (typically up to 8 years), after which we delete them.
  • Direct email correspondence is held within standard email systems subject to the same retention principles.

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with marketing partners or third-party advertisers.

Limited sharing may occur in these specific circumstances:

  • Email infrastructure: our email is hosted by a standard business email provider. They process email content as a technical necessity but do not access it for any other purpose.
  • Professional advisors: we may share information with our legal or accounting advisors where required for the conduct of the practice, under their own confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirement: where we are legally compelled to disclose information to a competent authority (court order, statutory requirement). We will narrow such disclosure to what is strictly required.

Cross-border transfers

We are based in India. Email infrastructure may involve servers in other jurisdictions. Where personal data of EU residents is involved, we rely on standard contractual safeguards required by GDPR. If you have specific concerns about cross-border processing of your personal data, contact us before submitting an enquiry.

Your rights

Under both DPDP and GDPR, you have rights regarding your personal data. These include:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your personal information, subject to any overriding legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent: if processing is based on consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Object: object to specific uses of your data.
  • Lodge a complaint: with India's Data Protection Board, or with your local supervisory authority in the EU.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@vnadvisory.in. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes restricting access to information to the principal advisor only, using secure email systems, and storing information on access-controlled devices. No internet transmission can be guaranteed perfectly secure, but we work to industry-standard practices.

Children

This website is intended for business audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information through this site, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, particularly when our practices change or when required by changes in applicable law. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through a clear notice on the site for a period before they take effect.
