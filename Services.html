Problem We Solve

Supply chain teams drowning in routine decisions — PO processing, carrier selection, stock replenishment, documentation. Humans making thousands of micro-decisions daily that follow predictable rules. The result: planner burnout, slow response times, and inconsistent execution.

Our Approach

Deploy an orchestrator that coordinates four specialized AI agents. Each agent handles a decision domain — procurement, inventory, logistics, trade — within governance boundaries you define. Connected to Tally, ERP, WMS, TMS, and external data APIs. Humans approve high-stakes decisions; AI handles the rest.

Agent Capabilities

Procurement: Autonomous PO, supplier scoring, price benchmarking
Inventory: Demand-driven replenishment, dead-stock ID, multi-location
Logistics: Carrier selection, route optimization, exception management
Trade: CBAM compliance, doc automation, chokepoint monitoring
Data Sources: Tally ERP WMS TMS Alpha Vantage GDELT
